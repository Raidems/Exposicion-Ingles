Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to your most common questions.

What are the requirements to enroll in a course?

+

Most of our courses do not require advanced prior knowledge, just a computer and internet connection. For more specialized courses like "Systems and Assembler," basic programming skills are recommended. Check each course page for specific requirements.

What is TechLearn Academy's teaching methodology?

+

Our methodology focuses on practical learning. We combine interactive live classes, support materials (videos, readings, exercises), real projects, and continuous instructor support to ensure you apply what you learn from day one.

Do I receive a certificate upon completing the courses?

+

Yes! All our courses include a completion certificate endorsed by TechLearn Academy, which you can share on your LinkedIn profile and with potential employers.

What kind of technical and academic support do you offer?

+

We offer support through discussion forums, live Q&A sessions, and direct communication channels with instructors. Our goal is for no student to be left with doubts.

Are there financing or scholarship options?

+

Currently, we do not offer direct financing or scholarships. However, we are working on partnerships to provide these in the future. Stay tuned to our social media.
